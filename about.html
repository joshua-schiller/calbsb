Who We Are

At our core, Business & Software at Berkeley (BSB) is a collection of students who have a shared interest in experiential learning and an enthusiasm to apply our knowledge to the real world. The value we bring our clients is a symptom of our desire to expand our backgrounds and satisfy our curiousity.

BSB is a consulting club and a coding club—we are professionally oriented—but we are also a uniquely tight-knit and trusting community. This is not only a benefit for the members of our club, but also a significant edge in our consulting and coding services; our propensity towards teamwork and collaboration shows in the quality our work.

As a club providing consulting and coding services, we are industry-agnostic. We are always exploring new verticals to dive into and new businesses to work with. BSB does not specialize in a specific industry—what unites the companies we have worked with is an opportunity to strengthen their business through our consulting and software engineering services. This is the opportunity we provide our members: the privilege to sit at a buffet of industries while developing and applying one's skills as a student. We believe the rigor and frequency of new projects at BSB is what makes this opportunity unique; post-graduation, it is rare to so swiftly learn as much about as many different businesses as our members do through their experiences.

Our Four Pillars

The defining principles of our culture.

Achieve Impeccable Quality

We set our standards high, then raise them. Relentlessly pursuing quality has enabled us to provide work on par with top consulting firms.

Embrace Diversity

We are a heterogeneous community. The varied backgrounds and experiences of our members are a big part of what allows for the unique insights in our solutions.

Be a Human, First

Skills are learned; character defines us. We believe traits like kindness, inclusivity, humor, and authenticity are the foundation for an organization creating meaningful impact.

Enjoy the Work

Passion fuels exceptional output. We staff our members such that their personal and professional interests align with our projects. We also like to have fun.

Engage With Us

If you are a student, we encourage you to start a conversation—we love meeting new people and are always scouting for potential members of the incoming BSB class. While we structure our recruitment cycle to take place at the beginning of each semester, we have coffee chats year-round. Pick someone from our team to talk with!

If you represent a company and are interested in learning more about BSB, we encourage you to reach out. We are always available for a noncommital discussion about what we have to offer and how we can help you and your organization.
