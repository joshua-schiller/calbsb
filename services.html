Our Approach

Discovery. Research & Analysis. Solutions.

While we adapt our methodology to client-specific needs, Business & Software at Berkeley approaches projects with structure. We use our semesterly recruitment cycle to tailor the background of our dedicated teams to each engagement's requirements.

After constructing the project scope with our client point of contact, we begin with Discovery—all team members dive deep into the project's areas of focus. Our goal in Discovery is to learn everything there is to know about your business.

Our teams then move into Research & Analysis, conducting targeted research, analyzing data, and developing software—everything that falls under the project scope. Research & Analysis is typically the longest phase, where the majority of our insights are captured and the groundwork for our project output is laid.

We conclude our engagements with Solutions. This is when our team finalizes their project output, whether it be strategy recommendations, functional software, design mockups, or more. Throughout the entire project, we emphasize iterative feedback, clear communication, and transparent collaboration.
