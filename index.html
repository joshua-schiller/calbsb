What We Do

While our range of work is broad, our expertise is most easily categorized into three areas:

Management Consulting

Providing traditional consulting services within marketing and growth, strategy, operations, and more.

Software Engineering

Building bespoke, robust software solutions tailored to complex technical challenges.

Product Management

Analyzing data and talking with customers to build, refine, and optimize products from end to end.

Who We Are

Business & Software at Berkeley (BSB) is an organization of UC Berkeley students dedicated to utilizing curiousity, grit, and creativity to solve real-world problems and provide real-world value. We recruit the top talent of our school to provide services of professional quality.
